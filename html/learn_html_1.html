The earliest discovered instance ... of well-established, settled agricultural society is at Mehrgarh in the hills between the Bolan Pass and the Indus plain (today in Pakistan) (see Map 3.1). From as early as 7000 BCE, communities there started sujal investing increased labor in preparing the land and selecting, planting, tending, ando harvesting particular grain-producing plants.

They also domesticated animals, including sheep, goats, pigs, and oxen (both humped zebu [Bos indicus] and unhumped [Bos taurus]). Castrating oxen, for instance, turned them from mainly meat sources into domesticated draft-animals as well.[37]

A paragraph is a series of sentences that are connected by transition words or phrases.
It is used to convey a coherent idea or a narrative to a reader. A good paragraph should have a clear thesis statement, which should be supported by the sentences that follow. The sentences in a paragraph should also be related to each other in terms of their content and flow smoothly from one to the other without interrupting the thought. Additionally, a good paragraph should be concise and to the point, avoiding unnecessary repetition or long-winded explanations. WHO
